Welcome to Olathe West High School, established in 2017, West emphasizes Project Based Learning (PBL) and has a wide variety of STEM related clubs, classes, and events. There are two CAD labs, two 21st Century Academies, technology integrated and centralized classes, and 11 Career and Technical Education (CTE) pathways. At Olathe West, we strive to expose students to unconventional learning, real-life situations/projects, and to help them find their interests.
